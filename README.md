# AI Image Prompts Library  

A curated collection of high-quality AI image prompts. This repository begins with an example showing the evolution of a man across 16 decades. Each mini-image is described in the prompt below.  

## Example: Evolution of a Man Across 16 Decades  

[Insert image here once uploaded]  

### Prompts  

#### Row 1 – Old Photography  

- **1880s**: Sepia daguerreotype, Victorian frock coat, mutton chops.  
- **1890s**: Faded albumen print, bowler hat and tweed suit.  
- **1900s**: Early black and white, Edwardian suit, industrial background.  
- **1910s**: Grainy WWI era photo, military tunic, somber expression.  

#### Row 2 – Classic Film  

- **1920s**: High contrast black & white, pinstripe suit, fedora, jazz vibe.  
- **1930s**: Depression-era black & white, rough work clothes, flat cap.  
- **1940s**: Film noir style, dramatic shadows, sharp suit.  
- **1950s**: Vintage color (Kodachrome), leather jacket, greaser hair.  

#### Row 3 – Retro Color  

- **1960s**: Faded retro color, Mod suit, mop‑top hair.  
- **1970s**: Warm yellow tones, long hair, corduroys.  
- **1980s**: Bright studio flash, neon background, power suit.  
- **1990s**: Harsh flash grunge style, flannel shirt, messy hair.  

#### Row 4 – Modern to Future  

- **2000s**: Early digital photo, frosted tips hair, baggy cargo pants.  
- **2010s**: High‑quality DSLR, hipster beard, coffee shop filter.  
- **2020s**: Sharp mirrorless portrait, clean home office, ring light.  
- **2030s**: Futuristic hyper‑realistic, tech‑wear, AR glasses interface.
